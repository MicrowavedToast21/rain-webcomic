About rain

Rain is a slice-of-life lgbtq+ webcomic written by trans author Jocelyn Samara DiDomenick. It was published in november 2010(Surprise, surprise, trans people existed back then!). The graphic novel is about the life of a teenage trans girl called Rain Flaherty who attends a private catholic high school and interacts with her friends and family around her as they come to terms with her new self.

Characters

Some of the wacky but wholesome characters include::
    • Rain Flaherty:

    Rain is the main protagonist. She is a very kind soul. She is a fan of Black Wings: Kaminari, an in-universe manga series. Rain moves from her hometown and enrols at St. Hallvard's in Centerville to be able to present femeninely. Her friend group and family accept her and support her.

    • Rudy Strongwell

    Rudy Strongwell is Marias gay brother. Ocasionally mischievous but deep down a really nice guy. He cross dresses as ruby sometimes.

    • Maria Strongwell

    Maria Strongwell is Rudy strongwells gay sister and also one of rain's best friends
